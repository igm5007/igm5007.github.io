TO THE CROWD

a neighborhood development program designed to create the new community of baseball

because it’s not just about the ballgame
IT'S ABOUT THE COMMUNITY
Today, a ballgame can be streamed, broadcast, and even live tweeted. But the crowd aspect of the baseball experience isn’t something that translates between cell towers. “Diehard” fans carry this sense of community with them, but for anyone else this communal feeling can only be found at the stadium on game day. “To The Crowd” extends this sense of community beyond the stadium walls by developing the areas around them.
